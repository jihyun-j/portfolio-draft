Overview

I had an opportunity that works as a UX design apprentice at BRIDGEGOOD. BRIDGEGOOD is a non-profit organization that supports design students develop their skills and industry knowledge. My teammates and I were assigned a design sprint to create the internship/entry-level job opportunities feature to provide inclusive and accessible job opportunities for entry-level design students. During work with my tames, I had a chance to do the user interview, wireframe, and prototype.

Problems

Job hunting is not easy for students since they lack experience. However, most companies prefer applicants with more than two years of experience, and they post many requirements on the job posting. It makes students overwhelmed and hesitant to apply. Students from the BRIDGEGOOD UX Design Apprenticeship have limited-to-no industry experience and want to gain real-world experience by getting hired for an internship.

Design Challenge

Designing an Internship/Entry Level Job Opportunities feature on BRIDGEGOOD.com, where companies can post available internship positions for students to apply to. BRIDGEGOOD can receive referral fees by connecting qualified students to companies.

Goals

Student

Provide accessible and inclusive opportunities for BRIDGEGOOD apprentices.

Company

Connecting talented creatives who have gone through an intensive UX Apprenticeship to internship and entry-level job opportunities.

BRIDGEGOOD

Have an automated/semi-automated referral process and gain revenue by allowing companies to post a job on our website.
