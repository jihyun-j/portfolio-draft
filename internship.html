Inclusive and safe job hunting experience for entry level design students.

Overview

BRIDGEGOOD is a non-profit organization that supports design students develop their skills and industry knowledge. We were assigned a design sprint to create the internship/entry-level job opportunities feature to provide inclusive and accessible job opportunities for entry-level design students.

Duration

3 days design sprint

Problems

Students from the BRIDGEGOOD UX Design Apprenticeship have limited-to-no industry experience and want to gain real-world experience by getting hired for an internship.

Challenge

Designing an Internship/Entry Level Job Opportunities feature on BRIDGEGOOD.com, where companies can post available internship positions for students to apply to. BRIDGEGOOD can receive referral fees by connecting qualified students to companies.

Goals

    Student

  • Provide accessible and inclusive opportunities for BRIDGEGOOD apprentices.

    Company

  • Connecting talented creatives who have gone through an intensive UX Apprenticeship to internship and entry-level job opportunities.

    Stakeholder

  • Have an automated/semi-automated referral process and gain revenue by allowing companies to post a job on our website.

Solution

Sign-up page

When users sign up for the app, the app will collect their personal and skin health information so that the app can provide the recommended cosmetic/skincare products on the home screen.

Home

  • It gives recommendation products so that users can explore quickly without spending time searching.
  • Review is the most critical consumers consideration, so I decided to add the most reviews/popular products on the home page.
  • I added an informative section to keep users engaged.

Search

  • Before searching for products or ingredients, users can discover the products when users don't have specific products to find.
  • Users can scan the products to check safety. Also, they can search products to check and compare safety, Price, and reviews for a final decision.

Product

  • It provides essential information about users' leading priorities, such as reviews and prices when purchasing beauty products.
  • It shows the summary of products safety to recognize whether the product safe or not quickly.

Reflection

Usability and accessibility are essential, but credibility is crucial:

This project is related to health, so credible organizations should verify the information so that users can trust the information and purchase the products.

Iteration, Iteration, and Iteration:

I had some new ambiguities and assumptions during the project, but it was helpful to back to the research phase and verify the hypotheses.

Listen to users:

I would like to hear from more users and test if I have a chance since users are the key to developing the products.
